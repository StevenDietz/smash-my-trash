01 — Introduction

Two receivables challenges hiding inside a 500-territory system

To the Smash My Trash leadership team — you have built the category leader in mobile waste compaction: 500+ franchised territories, a recurring-contract model, and a customer base of manufacturers, distributors, big-box retail, automotive and construction sites. That growth generates receivables risk in two places at once.

At the franchisor. Royalty and fee arrears, equipment-financing balances, and legacy balances from underperforming or terminated franchisees quietly erode the corporate P&L — and every failed franchise is a permanent hole in the royalty stream.

Across the network. Each franchisee bills small-balance, high-volume commercial invoices to slow-pay, project-driven and seasonal customers — construction general contractors chief among them — work that pulls owners and their staff off route and off sales.

Our role is not to replace anyone. It is to be discreet, contingency-only capacity for both — the aged, complex and relationship-sensitive balances that are hardest to chase efficiently. You pay nothing unless we recover, and every account is worked to preserve the commercial relationships your franchisees depend on next month.

05 — Built for franchise & service receivables

We speak your invoices

Recurring service billing across hundreds of independent operators creates a specific receivables profile. Our recovery playbook is built for exactly that.

Small-balance, high-volume AR

Thousands of sub-$1,000 service invoices are uneconomical for an internal team to chase — and exactly what our infrastructure liquidates at scale.

Slow-pay construction & seasonality

Project-based GCs and off-season lulls stretch terms for months. We recover the cash your franchisees have been financing for free.

Proof-of-service disputes

Your time-stamped smash photos and video are airtight evidence. We validate against them and close “we never got the service” disputes on the merits.

Multi-territory, multi-entity

One partner and one portal spanning 500+ franchises — role-based access, per-entity reporting, and a standard process where today there is none.

Best angle

The franchise A/R value-add — protect royalties, strengthen the network

The highest-leverage move is not just recovering corporate’s own arrears — it is offering Southwest Recovery to the entire network as an endorsed, zero-cost recovery benefit. Franchisees collect more of what they’re owed, their unit economics improve, fewer fail — and a healthier network protects the royalty stream that funds corporate.

What we do

Stand up a network-wide, contingency-only recovery program: franchisees place their aged commercial accounts through the portal, we work them under the SWRS compliance umbrella, and corporate recovers its own royalty and termination balances in parallel — all at no cost to the franchisor.

Where your counsel steps in

If a balance requires formal legal action or a franchise-agreement remedy, that stays with your franchise counsel. We hand off a fully documented, organized file and keep working recovery alongside them, so nothing stalls between the two.

This is the FRSTeam-style embedded-A/R model: a receivables partner offered through the franchisor becomes a genuine franchise-development selling point, not just a back-office fix.
